Grade 1 — Basic addition & subtraction

  1. 1) 5 + 3 = ?
    Show solution

    5 + 3 = 8

  2. 2) 10 - 4 = ?
    Show solution

    10 - 4 = 6

  3. 3) If you have 2 candies and get 5 more, how many candies do you have?
    Show solution

    2 + 5 = 7 candies.

Grade 2 — Introduction to addition & subtraction

  1. 1) 12 + 5 = ?
    Show solution

    12 + 5 = 17

  2. 2) 20 - 8 = ?
    Show solution

    20 - 8 = 12

  3. 3) If you have 10 apples and get 4 more, how many apples do you have?
    Show solution

    10 + 4 = 14 apples.

Grade 3 — Basic operations

  1. 1) 24 + 17 = ?
    Show solution

    24 + 17 = 41

  2. 2) 50 - 23 = ?
    Show solution

    50 - 23 = 27

  3. 3) If you have 4 packs of stickers and each pack has 9 stickers, how many stickers do you have?
    Show solution

    4 × 9 = 36 stickers.

Grade 4 — Multiplication & division

  1. 1) 8 × 7 = ?
    Show solution

    8 × 7 = 56

  2. 2) 56 ÷ 8 = ?
    Show solution

    56 ÷ 8 = 7

  3. 3) A ribbon 96 cm long is cut into 12 equal pieces. How long is each piece?
    Show solution

    96 ÷ 12 = 8 cm

Grade 5 — Fractions & decimals

  1. 1) What is 1/2 + 3/8? (Give answer as a fraction)
    Show solution

    1/2 = 4/8, so 4/8 + 3/8 = 7/8

  2. 2) Convert 0.75 to a fraction.
    Show solution

    0.75 = 75/100 = 3/4

  3. 3) If a pizza is cut into 8 slices and you eat 3 slices, what fraction is left?
    Show solution

    8 - 3 = 5 slices left → 5/8

Grade 6 — Ratios & basic algebra

  1. 1) If the ratio of cats to dogs is 3:5 and there are 15 dogs, how many cats are there?
    Show solution

    3/5 = cats/dogs → cats = 3/5 × 15 = 9

  2. 2) Solve for x: 4x = 28
    Show solution

    x = 28 ÷ 4 = 7

  3. 3) A shirt costs $24 after a 20% discount. What was the original price?
    Show solution

    If price after discount = 80% of original → original = 24 ÷ 0.8 = $30

Grade 7 — Pre-algebra & simple geometry

  1. 1) Solve: 3(x + 4) = 21
    Show solution

    Divide both sides by 3: x + 4 = 7 → x = 3

  2. 2) Find the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 6 cm.
    Show solution

    Area = 1/2 × base × height = 0.5 × 10 × 6 = 30 cm²

  3. 3) Evaluate: 2² + 3³
    Show solution

    2² = 4, 3³ = 27 → 4 + 27 = 31

Grade 8 — Linear equations & functions

  1. 1) Solve for y: 2y - 5 = 13
    Show solution

    2y = 18 → y = 9

  2. 2) If f(x) = 3x + 2, find f(4).
    Show solution

    f(4) = 3×4 + 2 = 14

  3. 3) A line has slope 2 and passes through (1,3). Write its equation.
    Show solution

    Point-slope: y - 3 = 2(x - 1) → y = 2x + 1

Grade 9 — Algebra & basic functions

  1. 1) Solve: x^2 − 5x + 6 = 0
    Show solution

    Factors to (x−2)(x−3)=0 → x=2 or x=3

  2. 2) Simplify: (3x^2)(2x^3)
    Show solution

    Multiply coefficients and add exponents: 6x^5

  3. 3) A line has slope −2 and passes through (1,4). Write its equation.
    Show solution

    y − 4 = −2(x − 1) → y = −2x + 6

Grade 10 — Algebra II / Geometry basics

  1. 1) Solve for x: 2x^2 − 8 = 0
    Show solution

    2x^2 = 8 → x^2 = 4 → x = ±2

  2. 2) Evaluate: log₁₀(1000)
    Show solution

    log₁₀(1000) = 3

  3. 3) Find sin(30°) (exact value)
    Show solution

    sin(30°) = 1/2

Grade 11 — Advanced algebra & precalc

  1. 1) Solve for x: 3e^(2x) = 24
    Show solution

    e^(2x) = 8 → 2x = ln 8 → x = (ln 8)/2

  2. 2) Expand: (x − 2)^3
    Show solution

    (x−2)^3 = x^3 − 6x^2 + 12x − 8

  3. 3) Sequence a_n = 2n + 1. What is a_10 and sum of first 10 terms?
    Show solution

    a_10 = 21. Sum = (10/2)*(a_1 + a_10) = 5*(3 + 21) = 120

Grade 12 — Precalculus / Intro to calculus

  1. 1) Find derivative: f(x) = 3x^2 − 5x + 4
    Show solution

    f'(x) = 6x − 5

  2. 2) Evaluate ∫₀² 2x dx
    Show solution

    Antiderivative x^2 → x^2|₀² = 4

  3. 3) Find lim_{x→2} (x^2 − 4)/(x − 2)
    Show solution

    Factor numerator: (x−2)(x+2)/(x−2) → limit = 4
